Welcome to Reality Filter

Your web browser is no longer a static window. It is a canvas. Let's customize your internet.

Under the Hood

This isn't just a simple dark mode extension. Reality Filter uses a custom-built Absolute Zero DOM Engine. It actively scans websites, bypasses complex Shadow DOMs, and neutralizes hardcoded gradients and backgrounds to force modern Web Apps to respect your aesthetic choices.

How to Hack Reality

1

Pin the Extension

Click the puzzle piece icon in your browser toolbar and pin Reality Filter so it's always ready.

2

Pick a Vibe

Click the icon and select a theme like Cyberpunk, Dark Academia, or Minimal Zen.

3

Set Immersion

Adjust the slider. Levels 3+ actively color-filter images. Level 5 triggers distraction-free Zen Mode.
